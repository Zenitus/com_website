STRIPE PAYOUT PROCESS

VOJVOJ currently supports Stripe payment to pay our creators. VOJVOJ will create your Stripe express account on the go and enable fund transfer. Click the Cash Out button on your wllet page to initiate the setup and payment process.

For additional support contact support@vojvoj.com

  • In VOJVOJ wallet page, click 'Cash Out' button to start setting up your account. If you have already completed setup, you will see the transfer screen directy.
  • Enter the Stripe email ID you wish to create and connect with VOJVOJ and click Next.
  • VOJVOJ will create the connected account in stripe. You need to complete the Onboarding process on Stripe website to continue. Click 'Stripe Onboarding' button to proceed.
  • The initial status will be 'Not Connected' and once you complete the onboarding, you can click 'Check Now' button to confirm Onboarding status.
Stripe Express Account Setup:
  • You will be taken to a browser window where you can complete the setup.
  • Enter your Email and Phone number and Submit.
Stripe OTP Verification:
  • You will be sent a OTP to your mobile number to Verify. Enter the OTP and Submit.
Personal details:
  • Enter your Name, DOB, Home address, Phone number and Submit.
Account for payouts:
  • To receive payouts, you’ll need to link your bank account with Stripe. Enter the details and Save.
Review and submit:
  • Once you review the details you have enetered, click 'Agree and submit'.
  • Go back to VOJVOJ app, and click 'Check Now' button. The account should be connected now with VOJVOJ. Click Next.
  • Just one more step !. VOJVOJ needs to confirm it is YOU the actual user who is trying to cash out. An OTP has been sent to your apple account registered email.
  • Sometimes, your email provider might categorise the OTP email as spam. Check your Spam folder to not miss it.
  • Enter the received OTP and click Confirm to finish the account setup.
Let's Cash Out:
  • Click the 'Cash Out' button in your Wallet page. You can enter the amount and initiate the payment.
Write to support@vojvoj.com for support.
